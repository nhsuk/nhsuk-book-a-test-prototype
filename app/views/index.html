You can get a test if you:

  • have coronavirus symptoms (a high temperature or a new, continuous cough)
  • live with someone who has coronavirus symptoms

Types of test

There are 2 types of test for coronavirus:

  • a test to see if you have coronavirus now (the antigen test)
  • a test when you get better to see if you had it (the antibody test)

The test you can request here is the antigen test. It involves taking a swab of your nose and the back of your throat. You can do it yourself or someone can do it for you.

Who can get a test

We are giving priority to testing people in hospital and testing key workers. You can find a list of key workers we are testing on GOV.UK.

But, as more tests become available, we are also offering tests to people who are self-isolating at home with coronavirus symptoms.
